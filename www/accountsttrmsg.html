IISWEB System

Help Info

You can review the trading strategy. The system provides the TR_NN1 and TR_NN2 Auto AI trading strategies to help you making the trading decision. You can also configure your account to do manual trading by yourself without auto AI support.

The ACCOUNT model is the normal trading transaction that can only support buy and sell.

The TR_NN1 and TR NN2 Neural Network models are using Neural Network AI to predict the best trading signal. The AI models are calculating the performance on both short selling or buying long transaction so that the result can cover price drop prediction.


Features:

  • ACCOUNT - Customer trading account
  • TR_NN1 - Auto AI trading strategy based on MACD signal for the neural network learning
  • TR_NN2 - Auto AI trading strategy based on EMA signal for the neural network learning
  • Configuration - configure the customer account to use manual trading or auto AI trading
