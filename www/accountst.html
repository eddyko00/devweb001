Help Info

You can add or delete stock symbol. For Canadian stock, you have to add '.TO' at the end of the stock symbol. e.g. RY.TO.

You can also search the stock symbol by Symbol Search


Field names:

  • Sig - Stock trading signal for Buy or Sell
  • Trend - Stock Trend direction from (-100 to 100)
  • Daily - Stock Daily percentage change
  • Perf - Account performance in 1 year



Note that for the fund manager account, the 4 best stocks will be selected automatically by the system from the trading account for the portfolio balance calculation.
