Field names:

  • Sig - Stock trading signal for Buy or Sell
  • Trend - Stock Trend direction from (-100 to 100)
  • Daily - Stock Daily percentage change
  • Perf - Account performance in 1 year



Note that for the fund manager account, the system will select 4 best stocks automatically from the trading account and they will be used for the portfolio balance calculation.
